# Roadmap: OpenLaunch

## Overview

This roadmap outlines our journey from initial community building to becoming a thriving collaborative innovation lab through our annual Coding Party initiatives. Our approach is iterative and community-driven, with regular checkpoints for feedback and course correction.

## Roadmap Principles

- **Community-First**: Every milestone prioritizes community growth and engagement
- **Quality over Speed**: We build sustainable, production-ready solutions
- **Iterative Development**: Regular releases with continuous feedback loops
- **Transparent Progress**: All progress is tracked and communicated openly
- **Flexible Planning**: Roadmap adapts based on community needs and learnings

## Project Selection Criteria

### Technical Criteria
- **Feasibility**: Can be built with available community skills
- **Scalability**: Architecture supports growth and evolution
- **Maintainability**: Sustainable long-term maintenance model
- **Innovation**: Incorporates new technologies or approaches
- **Quality**: Meets production-ready standards

### Community Criteria
- **Learning Value**: Provides growth opportunities for contributors
- **Collaboration Potential**: Enables meaningful teamwork
- **Mentorship Opportunities**: Allows experienced developers to guide newcomers
- **Diversity**: Appeals to contributors with different backgrounds and skills
- **Engagement**: Generates enthusiasm and sustained participation

### Impact Criteria
- **User Value**: Solves real problems for real users
- **Market Need**: Addresses underserved or emerging needs
- **Social Good**: Potential for positive societal impact
- **Open Source Alignment**: Advances open source ecosystem
- **Community Benefit**: Strengthens and grows our community
