# Events: Coding Party 2026

## Overview

Events are the heartbeat of our community. They bring us together to learn, collaborate, celebrate achievements, and build lasting relationships. From regular virtual meetups to annual conferences, our events are designed to be inclusive, educational, and fun.

## Upcoming Events

### Regular Events

#### Weekly Community Sync
**When**: Every Tuesday at 7:00 PM UTC  
**Duration**: 1 hour  
**Format**: Virtual (Zoom)  
**What**: Weekly check-in on project progress, community updates, and open discussion

- New contributor introductions
- Community announcements
- Open floor for questions and discussion
- Breakout rooms for specific topics

**How to Join**: [Community Calendar Link] | [Zoom Link]

## Event Guidelines

### Participation Guidelines

#### For All Events
- **Be Respectful**: Follow our Code of Conduct
- **Be Prepared**: Read agendas and come ready to participate
- **Be Engaged**: Participate actively and constructively
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome newcomers and different perspectives

#### For Presenters
- **Be Clear**: Use clear, accessible language
- **Be Concise**: Respect time limits and stay on topic
- **Be Interactive**: Engage your audience
- **Be Prepared**: Test technology and have backups ready

#### For Organizers
- **Be Organized**: Plan thoroughly and communicate clearly
- **Be Accessible**: Ensure events are accessible to all
- **Be Responsive**: Address questions and issues promptly
- **Be Reflective**: Gather feedback and improve continuously

### Accessibility

We are committed to making all events accessible:

#### Virtual Event Accessibility
- **Closed Captions**: Available for all video events
- **Screen Reader Compatibility**: Accessible presentation formats
- **Multiple Time Zones**: Recordings available for different schedules
- **Bandwidth Considerations**: Low-bandwidth options available

#### Physical Event Accessibility
- **Venue Accessibility**: Wheelchair accessible venues
- **Dietary Accommodations**: Various dietary options available
- **Assistive Technology**: Support for hearing and visual aids
- **Quiet Spaces**: Available for those who need breaks

### Code of Conduct

All events are governed by our [Code of Conduct](code-of-conduct.md). Key points:

- **Respectful Communication**: Professional and inclusive language
- **No Harassment**: Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- **Constructive Participation**: Focus on learning and collaboration
- **Report Issues**: Mechanisms available for reporting problems
